About the Game



Keith is a browser game in which you play as Fang, an anthropomorphic fox. He has a foster child called Keith, who is also an anthropomorphic fox. Fang becomes stronger the better his bond with Keith becomes. So, you'll have to have deep conversations with Keith in order to improve your relationship with him, and so that you can defeat enemies more easily.

Your objective is to defeat all the enemy robots that will show up onscreen, and to level up by talking to Keith.

This game was made by Eduardo Salinas for for CS50's Web Programming with Python and JavaScript's final project.
